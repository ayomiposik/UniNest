Smart Student Housing Tips 💡

Check What's Included

Always ask if utilities (electricity, water, internet) are included in the monthly rent baseline to avoid unexpected bills.

Understand the Lease

Don't sign blindly. Ensure you know if you are signing an individual lease or a joint lease with roommates.

Inspect Before Moving In

Take photos of any existing damage on your move-in day so you don't lose your security deposit later.
